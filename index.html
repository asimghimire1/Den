WHAT DOES ZERO LOOK LIKE?

A Simple Process to connect startup with the right Investors

STEP 1:

DISCOVER & JOIN

For Startups: Create a profile showcasing your ideas, team, and vision.

For Investors: Set up your preferences to find startups aligned with your interests.

STEP 2:

SHARE & EXPLORE

For Startups: Post updates about your progress, challenges, and achievements to attract interest.

For Investors: Browse startup ideas, review pitches, and engage with entrepreneurs.

STEP 3:

CONNECT & ENGAGE

For Startups: Start conversations with potential investors, respond to comments, and build meaningful relationships.

For Investors: Provide constructive feedback, network with founders, and discover hidden opportunities.

STEP 4:

COLLAB & GROW

For Startups: Apply for funding by sending your profile and business plans directly to interested investors.

For Investors: Use tools to evaluate startups, track progress, and facilitate partnerships.

STEP 5:

ACHIEVE & SCALE

For Startups: Utilize resources to scale your business, implement investor recommendations, and measure success.

For Investors: Monitor ROI, mentor startups, and contribute to their long-term growth.

Frequently
Asked
Questions

What is Zero?
Zero is a platform for entrepreneurs, startup founders, and investors. It’s where innovative startup ideas meet funding. Founders pitch their ventures, and investors discover promising startups to back—building a thriving startup community together.
Is it free to join Zero?
Yes, joining Zero is free for startups looking to connect with investors.
How can I pitch my startup on Zero?
You can pitch your startup by creating a profile on Zero and submitting your pitch deck through the platform.
How do investors find startups to invest in?
Investors can browse startup profiles on Zero, filter by industry, and reach out to founders directly.
Is Zero involved in the investment process?
Zero facilitates connections but is not directly involved in the investment process or negotiations.
Can I get feedback on my idea without pitching for funding?
Yes, Zero offers feedback options through its community features, allowing you to get insights from experts.
